Services We Offer

At Slip Safe Colorado, we provide innovative solutions that make indoor and outdoor spaces safer for everyone. Our cutting-edge treatments reduce the risk of slip and falls while maintaining the original appearance of your surfaces.

Non-Slip Floor Treatment

Our Sure Step treatment creates an invisible micro tread design on hard mineral floors, increasing slip-resistance. The treatment increases the dynamic coefficient of friction when a floor or surface gets wet, making it dramatically more slip-resistant and safer. Suitable for concrete, Spanish tile, glazed brick, terrazzo, quarry tile, ceramic tile, marble or porcelain/enamel tile.

Non-Slip Bathtub Treatment

Our specialized bathtub treatment provides a safer bathing experience for you and your family. The treatment leaves no residue, film, or coating, maintaining the original appearance of your bathtub. Applied by certified installers, it dramatically increases slip-resistance when wet, providing peace of mind and a secure surface.

Slip Testing (DCOF Measurement)

Using the BOT-3000E digital floor tribometer, we measure the Dynamic Coefficient of Friction (DCOF) on your floors. Slip resistance testing can detect problems before they cause accidents and verify that treatments are effective. Monitoring tests are typically conducted quarterly, semiannually, or annually to ensure ongoing safety compliance.

Tile and Grout Deep Clean

Our comprehensive tile and grout deep cleaning service removes built-up dirt, grime, and stains while restoring your surfaces to their original beauty. This service can be combined with our slip-resistant treatments for a complete floor safety and maintenance solution. Professional caulking services are also available.

What kind of surface can this be applied to?

Our Sure Step treatment is applied by our certified installers and then promptly removed, leaving zero residue, film or coating. Our products will not change or alter the appearance of the treated floor or bathtub.
